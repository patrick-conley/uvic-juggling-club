Public Show:

 

 

An International Showcase of Juggling and the Performing Arts

This show is going to rock so hard that actual rocks will sprout spontaneously from the stage. The Victoria Juggling Festival Public Show features a wide variety of circus acts, including comedy, contact juggling, poi, rola bola, hoops and juggling. 11 performers from Vancouver Island and the mainland will present their talents in this cabaret-style production. We bring to Victoria a night of incredible circus flair, witty comedy and dazzling juggling feats.

Performers include

 

Tony Duncan         
Matt Cantrell           Matt Baker
Curt Carlye   Aaron Gregg      
Ryan Mellors
Blake Speers       ... and a few surprises!
