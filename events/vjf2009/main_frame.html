Who Will Be There?

Our star attraction is IJA Gold Medalist Tony Duncan Who will be doing multiple acts in our Public Show as well as teaching workshops. Curt Carlyle is coming all the way from Portland, Oregon and hopefully will bring his IJA Bronze Medal to show off. Guinness Record Holder and America's Got Talent competitor Matt Baker is joining us from Seattle.

Rounding out the cast are the VJF usual suspect including contact juggling wonderman Ryan Mellors of the Ministry of Manipulation, drywall enthusiast Matt Cantrell and of course chainsaw juggling and plunger throwing Aaron Gregg.
