Workshops

The Victoria Juggling Festival will be offering a wide range of free workshops for beginner to advanced jugglers. Some of the best jugglers in North America will be teaching; this is your chance to pick up new skills in Contact Juggling, Diabolo, Club Passing, Ball Juggling, Cigar boxes, Passing and more!

A workshop sign-up board will be available at the festival listing the workshop times. If you have something you would like to teach, sign up! Remember, festival entrance and workshops are all FREE!
